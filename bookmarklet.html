One-click job capture

Add this bookmarklet to your browser. Whenever you're on a LinkedIn job page, click it and the job details fill in automatically.

Drag the red button above into your browser bookmarks bar

2
Go to any LinkedIn job page

Open a job listing on LinkedIn. It works on both the job detail page and Easy Apply pages.

3
Click "Log to Orbit" in your bookmarks bar

A small popup appears pre-filled with the company name, role, and today's date. Review, add any notes, and save.

What it does
  • Reads the job title and company name from the page
  • Also works on Indeed, Naukri, Glassdoor, and most job sites
  • Opens a small popup where you can add status, source, and notes
  • Saves directly to your Firestore — appears on the board instantly
  • No install, no extension, no login needed in the popup

The bookmarklet uses your existing Firebase session from Orbit.
Make sure you're signed into Orbit in another tab before using it.
